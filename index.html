About RecipeGen

A Benchmark Dataset for Recipe Image Generation

RecipeGen is a comprehensive dataset designed to advance research in recipe image generation. It features a diverse collection of recipes with step-by-step images, aimed at improving AI models' ability to generate realistic and contextually accurate culinary visuals.
